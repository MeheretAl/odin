Shiro

Recipe

We first chop up the onions and put them in a pan with oil and we let that cook for a couple of minutes after which we put pepper in it and we again wait a couple of minutes before we add water.

We let the mixture boil after which we add the shiro powder and then we stir for some time ranging from 10 mins to an hour or two depending on the amount of food we're cooking.

    Ingredients

  • Shiro Powder
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Garlic(Optionary)
  • Water
